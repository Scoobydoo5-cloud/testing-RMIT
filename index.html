Random Facts About RMIT

  • Founded in 1887 in Melbourne, Australia.
  • One of the world's leading global universities of technology, design, and enterprise.
  • Over 85,000 students enrolled worldwide.
  • Strong emphasis on creativity, innovation, and real-world experience.
  • Campus includes several modern and sustainable buildings.

Highlighted Academic Programs

  • Engineering and Technology – cutting-edge research and training.
  • Design – award-winning programs in fashion, graphic, and industrial design.
  • Business and Management – strong industry links and internships.
  • Health and Biomedical Sciences – modern labs and research facilities.
  • Media and Communication – hands-on learning with leading tools.

Campus Life at RMIT

  • Vibrant multicultural student community.
  • Wide range of clubs, sports, and cultural activities.
  • Access to top-notch libraries, cafes, and study spaces.
  • Strategically located campuses in Melbourne city center and beyond.
  • Dedicated support services for student success and wellbeing.
